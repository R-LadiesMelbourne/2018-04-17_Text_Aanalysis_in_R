Information in this package is publicly available at https://www.kaggle.com/wcukierski/the-simpsons-by-the-data/version/1#_=_

The dataset contains the characters, locations, episode details, and script lines for approximately 600 Simpsons episodes, dating back to 1989.

Inspiration and credit for gathering the data goes to Todd Schneider:
http://toddwschneider.com/posts/the-simpsons-by-the-data/ 
https://github.com/toddwschneider/flim-springfield

The tasks are adapted from https://www.kaggle.com/ambarish/fun-in-text-mining-with-simpsons
